Global Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global sales performance, identify key trends, and generate business insights. It enables data-driven decision-making through visual analytics and KPI tracking.

Key Objectives
•	Analyze overall sales, profit, and order volume
•	Track profit margins and category performance
•	Identify top-performing products and regions
•	Monitor sales trends over time
•	Compare market performance across regions
•	Understand customer segment distribution

Dashboard Highlights
•	Total Sales: ₹12.64M
•	Total Profit: ₹1.47M
•	Total Orders: 25K
•	Profit Margin Analysis
•	Region-wise Sales Distribution
•	Category-wise Performance
•	Sales Trend Over Time
•	Customer Segment Insights

Tools & Technologies Used
•	Power BI – Data Visualization & Dashboard Creation
•	Excel / CSV – Data Source
•	DAX – Calculations & Measures
•	Power Query – Data Cleaning & Transformation

Key Insights
•	Technology category contributes significantly to overall profit
•	Consumer segment drives the highest sales share
•	North America and Europe are top-performing regions
•	Sales show a steady upward trend over the years
